About Sai Baba

üôè

Sai Baba of Shirdi, also known as Shirdi Sai Baba, was an Indian spiritual master who is regarded by his devotees as a saint, fakir, and satguru.

He preached the importance of self-realization, criticized love towards perishable things, and his teachings concentrate on a moral code of love, forgiveness, helping others, charity, contentment, inner peace, and devotion to God and guru.

Core Teachings: Faith (Shraddha) and Patience (Saburi)

Temple Services

Daily Aarti

Join us for daily morning and evening aarti ceremonies filled with devotional music and prayers.

Sai Satcharitra Reading

Weekly readings from the sacred Sai Satcharitra, the biography of Sai Baba's life and teachings.

Prasad Distribution

Sacred prasad is distributed daily to all devotees after the aarti ceremonies.

Special Pujas

Book special pujas for occasions like birthdays, anniversaries, and festivals.

Meditation Hall

A peaceful space for meditation and silent contemplation open throughout the day.

Annadaan
